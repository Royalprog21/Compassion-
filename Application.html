About us

    Compassion International is a Christ-centered organization engaged in transformative child advocacy and holistic child development, particularly for children living in extreme poverty. Our visioin is dervied from the parlble of the Good Samaritan told by Jesus in the book of Luke. Although Samaritans and Jews despised each other, the Samaritan helps the injured man. At compassion, we desire to help and show love to all without bias or expecting anything in return. We are devoted to help children of all faiths, culture, backgrounds and race - without imposing any religious obligation.
